Scenario

Picture this. You're browsing on the internet and you suddenly find that you can't access your banking account. You feel like everything is doomed.

Identity theft can't only happen with the loss of your wallet.

What is identity theft?

Identity theft is a serious crime, and it is one where your personal information has been jeopardized.

Well, I don't have anything to hide. Why does it matter?

The fraudster can rack up significant debt in your name, leave you with a criminal record, and do much more with just your Social Insurance Number.
